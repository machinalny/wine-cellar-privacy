# Wine Cellar — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

---

Wine Cellar does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## What stays on your device

Your wine collection — names, vintages, ratings, tasting notes, and label photos — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It is synced to your personal iCloud account via CloudKit. This data never passes through any server operated by the developer. It goes directly from your device to your own iCloud private database, which only you can access.

## What the app uses

| Feature | What it accesses | Where data goes |
|---|---|---|
| iCloud / CloudKit | Your private iCloud database | Your iCloud account only |
| Camera | Captures label photos | Stored on your device and iCloud |
| Photo Library | Selects existing photos for labels | Stored on your device and iCloud |
| On-device OCR (Apple Vision) | Reads text from label photos | Processed on-device; never leaves your device |
| UserDefaults | Sort order preference, milestone flags | On-device only |

## What the app does not do

- No analytics or usage tracking
- No advertising or ad networks
- No third-party SDKs
- No account creation or sign-in
- No servers or APIs operated by the developer

## Children's privacy

Wine Cellar does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

If the privacy practices change in a future version (for example, when a backend or AI features are introduced), this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date will change.
